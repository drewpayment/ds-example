What is Geofencing?

A geofence is a virtual boundary around a specified location that records when someone on a mobile device enters or exits that boundary in the physical world. In Dominion, geofences are determined and maintained by company administrators to capture punch locations for employees. Once an employee enters a punch, the punch time and location data is collected and saved so you can see where your employees are punching. Geofencing provides your employees with a safe, personal punch method instead of relying on kiosk devices. Geofencing works great for construction, sales, home care, or any employee that needs to record time from multiple locations.
